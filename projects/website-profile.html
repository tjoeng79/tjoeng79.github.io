About

A landing page is a standalone web page created specifically for a marketing campaign, where visitors "land" after clicking on a link from an email, ad, or other online source.

Its primary purpose is to drive conversions by encouraging users to take a specific action, such as signing up for a newsletter or making a purchase.

Landing pages often feature compelling headlines, concise messaging, and persuasive calls to action, with a layout designed to minimize distractions.

Landing Page: Single goal, minimal navigation, campaign-specific traffic, focused content, designed for conversion.

Homepage: Multiple goals, full navigation, diverse traffic sources, broad content, designed for exploration and brand engagement.

Understanding these differences helps businesses optimize user experience and conversion rates by directing visitors to the appropriate page based on their intent and source of traffic
